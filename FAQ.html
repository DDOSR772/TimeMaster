Frequently Asked Questions

What is this timer for?

This timer can be used for various activities such as workouts, speedruns, cooking, and more. It tracks time accurately and displays it in a leaderboard format.

How do I start and stop the timer?

You can start and stop the timer by pressing the spacebar or clicking the start/stop button.

How does the leaderboard work?

The leaderboard records your times whenever you stop the timer. It sorts the times from fastest to slowest.

How is my time saved?

Your time is saved in your browser's local storage and is specific to your IP address. If you use a different computer or network, you will not see your previous times.
